About Us

We Care for You

India, despite notable progress in its immunization program, continues to grapple with delayed and incomplete child vaccinations, leading to a significant portion of children being vulnerable to vaccine-preventable diseases. To address this critical issue and further strengthen the nation's vaccination efforts, there is a pressing need for the development of an efficient and user-friendly child vaccination management system that can bridge the existing gaps, reduce delays, and ensure that every child receives timely and complete vaccinations.
To keep a child healthy and secure from future risks of deadly diseases, a proper vaccination schedule should be maintained. But many parents do not know about the schedule or they forget about it and consequently their child becomes vulnerable to diseases. To prevent child-death solely for this reason, a system should be placed to notify the parents prior to vaccination schedule by mobile SMS or in other ways.
